Common Exercises

The follwoing are common excersises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are great, full body exercise to increase your strenght and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump up from a sqauted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strenght. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Moutain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can (as if you are climbing a mountain).
